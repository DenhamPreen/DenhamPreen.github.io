EduFund

a swipe to micro fund education app

Problem Statement

There are two ways to impact employment, either job creation through an open market platform like that of uber or upskill people. This comes down to a prevalent issue of access to education. How do you improve access to education? With technology as its ability to scale at no additional cost. Online facilities are underutilised. Therefore, how can we make current education more accessible? By making it more affordable. A popular opinion is that the government has the ability to fund free education but is unable because funds are misallocated. This app is a platform that allows people to donate micro payments towards paying for individuals education. A platform that links students to funders, where the key is that a funder can fund small amounts less than R5.

6%

The percentage of tax revenue spent on post school education and training.

10%

The percentage of Income Tax that can be deducted for charitable donations.

What if you could choose where 10% of your taxable income went?

You can and its perfectly legal. 10% of your taxable income can be allocated to registered charities

What?

An app that lets you support a student while choosing where 10% of your taxable income goes

How?

Through a mobile platform that links people with charitable intentions to students that don't have the means to support their funds

Why?

Education is the foundation of development. Education is too expensive in South Africa. Its reserved for the wealthy and intrinsically discriminatory. The wealthy get wealthier and the poor get poorer. Why is education not affordable? is it because the government cant afford to build a foundation for the future? Or is it because the government is "misallocating" those funds away from a prosperous future?
